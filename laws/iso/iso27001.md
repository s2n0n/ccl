# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Information Security Management System (ISMS) Compliance Reference

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 specifies the requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and
continually improving an information security management system. Annex A provides a reference
set of controls including those governing information classification, data masking, access
control, and privacy protection of personal data.

---

## Annex A.5.12 — Classification of Information

**Summary:** Information shall be classified according to legal requirements, value, criticality
and sensitivity to unauthorised disclosure or modification. Datasets containing personal data
(PII) must be identified and classified to apply appropriate handling controls.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A5.12-001
- severity: LOW
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.5.12 (Classification of Information)
- description: Dataset contains personal data (PII) — information classification review required under ISMS policy
- condition: pii_present()
- action: UNKNOWN

---

## Annex A.5.31 — Legal, Statutory, Regulatory and Contractual Requirements

**Summary:** The organisation shall identify, document and keep up-to-date its legal, statutory,
regulatory and contractual requirements with relevance to information security. Processing of
personal data without a documented and specified purpose breaches this control.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A5.31-001
- severity: HIGH
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.5.31 (Legal, Statutory, Regulatory and Contractual Requirements)
- description: Processing purpose is not specified — purpose limitation is required by ISMS legal compliance controls
- condition: purpose_unspecified()
- action: FAIL

---

## Annex A.5.33 — Protection of Records

**Summary:** Records shall be protected from loss, destruction, falsification, unauthorised
access and unauthorised release. Special category data (health, biometric, political beliefs)
stored without masking violates the integrity and confidentiality requirements of this control.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A5.33-001
- severity: HIGH
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.5.33 (Protection of Records)
- description: Sensitive data (health, biometric, political) found without masking — violates ISMS record protection control
- condition: any_column(category='sensitive', masked=False)
- action: FAIL

---

## Annex A.5.34 — Privacy and Protection of Personal Information

**Summary:** The organisation shall identify and meet the requirements regarding the preservation
of privacy and protection of personal information as required by applicable laws and regulations
and contractual requirements. Absence of consent records indicates a failure of this control.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A5.34-001
- severity: HIGH
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.5.34 (Privacy and Protection of Personal Information)
- description: Consent records are absent — personal data processing without documented consent violates ISMS privacy control
- condition: consent_missing()
- action: FAIL

---

## Annex A.8.11 — Data Masking

**Summary:** Data masking shall be used in accordance with the organisation's topic-specific
policy on access control and other related topic-specific policies, and business requirements,
taking applicable legislation into consideration. Direct identifiers (email, phone, IP addresses)
must be masked when not necessary for the processing purpose.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A8.11-001
- severity: MEDIUM
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.8.11 (Data Masking)
- description: Direct identifiers (email, phone, IP) are not masked — violates ISMS data masking control
- condition: any_column(category='direct_identifier', masked=False)
- action: FAIL

---

## Annex A.8.12 — Data Leakage Prevention

**Summary:** Data leakage prevention measures shall be applied to systems, networks and any
other devices that process, store or transmit sensitive information. Unique government identifiers
(SSN, passport numbers, national ID) stored without encryption represent a high-risk leakage
vector that must be controlled.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A8.12-001
- severity: HIGH
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.8.12 (Data Leakage Prevention)
- description: Unique identifiers (SSN, passport, national ID) are not encrypted — violates ISMS data leakage prevention control
- condition: any_column(category='unique_id', encrypted=False)
- action: FAIL

---

## Annex A.8.10 — Information Deletion

**Summary:** Information stored on information systems, devices or in any other storage media
shall be deleted when no longer required. A retention period exceeding 3 years (1095 days)
without documented justification indicates a failure to enforce information deletion policies
under the ISMS framework.

**Validation Rule Mapping:**
- rule_id: ISO27001-A8.10-001
- severity: HIGH
- article: ISO 27001:2022 Annex A.8.10 (Information Deletion)
- description: Data retention period exceeds ISMS information deletion baseline (1095 days / 3 years)
- condition: retention_exceeded(days=1095)
- action: FAIL
